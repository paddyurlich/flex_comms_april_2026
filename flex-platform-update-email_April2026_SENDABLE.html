New on Flex: richer energy charts, battery state of charge, and financial reporting are now live on your dashboard.
 
 

Flex Platform  ·  April 2026

New features live on
your Energy Dashboard.

We've upgraded the Energy Dashboard with deeper chart visibility, battery charge tracking, and financial reporting, giving you a clearer picture of how your system is performing and what it's worth.

 
 
 
01    

Energy Dashboard Update

Power charts: all six measures plus battery state of charge

The power chart on your Energy Dashboard now shows everything at a glance: what you’re pulling from the grid, sending back, generating yourself, and using on-site, along with energy flowing in and out of your battery. A second line shows how full your battery is at any point in the day.

This gives you the full operational picture in one place: how energy is flowing through your system and how your battery is responding.

 
 
02    

Energy Dashboard Update

Daily and longer-period energy views

Select a time period greater than a single day and the chart automatically switches to display Energy (kWh) rather than instantaneous power. Compare day-by-day performance across a week or month, tracking generation totals, identifying patterns and understanding how your system performs over time.

 
 
03    

New Feature

Energy financial reporting: cost and revenue in dollar terms

Flex now supports Energy Financial Reporting. Once a tariff is assigned to your site, the platform automatically calculates and displays two key financial metrics for any selected period.

Import Cost

Your grid import volume calculated against your configured tariff rate.

  

Export Revenue

Revenue from energy exported to the grid, tracked against your export rate.
